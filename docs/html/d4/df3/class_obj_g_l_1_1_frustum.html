Construct a frustum in a particular space for isVisible2Sided culling.

The frustum space is determined by the provided viewTransform. Frustum tests will evaluate spheres provided in a space mapped to eye space by the inverse view transform (Peval = iVT*Peye). Constructs frustum looking in positive z direction.

Parameters
fovX_degfield of view in x dimension of eye space in degrees
fovY_degfield of view in y dimension of eye space in degrees
viewTransformtransform evaluation space to eyespace; if I (default) frustum is defined in eye space
